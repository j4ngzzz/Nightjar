# Contributor Covenant Code of Conduct

## Our Pledge

We pledge to make participation in this project a harassment-free experience for everyone, regardless of age, body size, disability, ethnicity, sex characteristics, gender identity, level of experience, education, socio-economic status, nationality, personal appearance, race, religion, or sexual identity and orientation.

## Our Standards

Examples of behavior that contributes to a positive environment:

- Being direct and specific in technical discussions
- Accepting that different people make different tradeoffs, and that's usually fine
- Giving and receiving feedback on code without making it personal
- Acknowledging when you were wrong
- Asking before assuming

Examples of unacceptable behavior:

- Harassment, public or private
- Publishing someone's private information without permission
- Personal attacks or sustained negativity directed at a person rather than their code
- Conduct that would be unwelcome in a professional setting

## Enforcement

Project maintainers will remove, edit, or reject contributions that don't align with this code of conduct. Repeat or severe violations may result in a temporary or permanent ban.

Report issues to: nightjar-license@proton.me
